Algebraic formulation

Work done is equal to change in mechanical energy, $$ dW = dK + dU $$

Implies, $$ P_1A_1dx_1 - P_2A_2dx_2 = (\frac{1}{2}\rho v^2_2dx_2A_2 - \frac{1}{2}\rho v^2_1dx_1A_1) + (\rho gh_2dx_2A_2 - \rho gh_1dx_1A_1) $$
$$ (P_1 + \frac{1}{2}\rho v^2_1 + \rho gh_1)dx_1A_1 = (P_2 + \frac{1}{2}\rho v^2_2 + \rho gh_2)dx_2A_2 $$
\( \text{since} \) $$ dx_1A_1 = dx_2A_2 = dV \text{ (incompressible fluid)} $$
$$ P + \frac{1}{2}\rho v^2 + \rho gh = constant $$
