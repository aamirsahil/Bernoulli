Calculating the change in kinetic energy

  • The position of the fluid element:
    • At \(t\) is ① + ⊛
    • At \(t + dt\) is ⊛ + ②;
  • Implies initial \(KE\) \(= KE_1 + KE_*\)
  • Final \(KE = KE_* + KE_2\)
$$ \Delta KE = KE_2 - KE_1 = \frac{1}{2}m_2v_2^2 - \frac{1}{2}m_1v_1^2 $$
where, \(m_1 = \rho A_1ds_1 \text{ and } m_2 = \rho A_2ds_2\)
