Work done

  • We focus our analysis on an element of fluid.
The position of the fluid element at \(t\) is ①
The position of the fluid element at \(t + dt\) is ②
  • Force is exerted on the fluid element by the pressure of the surrounding fluid
Force along the motion \( = P_1 A_1 \implies \) $$ \text{work done on the fluid element} = P_1 A_1 ds_1 $$
Force against the motion \( = P_2 A_2 \implies \) $$ \text{work done by the fluid element} = P_2 A_2 ds_2 $$
