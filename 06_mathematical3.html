Lift on Air foil

Now lets look at the original problem of airplane lift qualitatively.

  • We consider streamlines above and below the airfoil.
  • Streamlines above the foil is streamline A.
  • Streamlines above the foil is streamline B.
  • The shape of the streamline determines the velocity of fluid elements.
  • Velocity above \(>\) Velocity below.
  • Thus Pressure above \(<\) Pressure below.
  • Hence a net lift is experienced.
